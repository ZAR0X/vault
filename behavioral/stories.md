# Behavioral / STAR Stories

"Tell me about a time…" — you have *real* material here; most students fake it. Draft each as **S**ituation · **T**ask · **A**ction · **R**esult. (We write these out in Phase 3 — for now, just know the raw material is captured.)

## Raw material → turn into STAR later
1. **CatUserBot at scale** — 15k+ users, 99% uptime, 50+ reviews. Angles: a nasty bug debugged across distributed user-deployed setups; improving DB reliability; reviewing/mentoring contributors.
2. **Hackathons (8x)** — ByteQuest 1st runner-up out of 400+ teams: shipping under extreme time pressure. **This is literally your superpower — sell it as a strength, not a confession.**
3. **Head of Coding Club** — leadership, organizing workshops, mentoring juniors.
4. **A real tradeoff** — RAG chunking vs retrieval latency, or the React-Context Window Manager architecture in the OS portfolio.
